## ğŸ¯ Why Recall Matters More Than Accuracy in Medical AI
In medical risk assessment, **recall is more important than accuracy**.

A **false negative** means a diabetic patient is incorrectly classified as healthy, which can delay diagnosis and treatment.

- Logistic Regression offers balanced accuracy
- Naive Bayes achieves **very high recall**, meaning it rarely misses actual diabetes cases

â¡ For **early-stage diabetes screening**, a model with higher recall is preferred even if overall accuracy is lower.
